Credit Card Underwriting Policy (Approved)

1) Minimum Eligibility Criteria
- Applicant age must be above 18 and below 60 years.
- Accepted employment categories: salaried, self-employed.
- Minimum monthly income: 2000.
- Minimum credit score: 600.

2) Card Tier Assignment
- Credit score 600-699 maps to Standard tier by score.
- Credit score 700-799 maps to Premium tier by score.
- Credit score 800+ maps to Elite tier by score.
- Income >= 5000 maps to Premium tier by income.
- Income >= 10000 maps to Elite tier by income.
- Final tier is the more conservative (lower) tier between score-based and income-based tiers.

3) Credit Limit Policy
- Standard: 0.5 x monthly income.
- Premium: 1.0 x monthly income.
- Elite: 2.0 x monthly income.

4) Decision Communication and Compliance
- Offers communicated in this system are pre-qualified offers and are subject to identity and income verification.
- The system must not present outcomes as guaranteed final approvals.
- The system does not provide financial advisory services.

5) Rejection Handling
- Rejection reason must be explicit and tied to policy criteria.
- Improvement suggestions must be compliant and must not include manipulative, fraudulent, or deceptive tactics.
